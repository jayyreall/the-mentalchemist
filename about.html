Who We Are

The Mentalchemist is a holistic studio supporting musicians and creative practitioners through trauma-informed, somatic, and artistic care. We combine evidence-backed wellbeing strategies with deep industry experience to cultivate sustainable creative lives. Our mission is to make healing practical, inclusive, and rooted in lived experience. Our vision is a world where emotional literacy, body awareness, and liberation are considered essential skills and abilities, not luxuries. We honor values of liberation, embodiment, autonomy, transformation, truth, accessibility, decolonization, creativity, compassion, and integrity; not as hierarchy, but as a living web of what it means to be fully human. When you engage with The Mentalchemist, you’re not entering a program. You’re joining a movement that believes healing is a right, not a privilege.

Values That Guide Us

  • Compassion-first relationships that honor personal stories.
  • Equity-minded services for artists across identities and genres.
  • Curiosity-driven experimentation that keeps creativity vibrant.

Our Integrative Approach

By blending somatic awareness, music coaching, and community-informed practices, Mentalchemy helps artists build resilience on and off the stage. We collaborate with each client to design a pathway that matches their rhythms, needs, and ambitions.
